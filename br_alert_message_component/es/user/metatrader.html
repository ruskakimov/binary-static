Choose an account

Binary.com offers a variety of account types to cater to the diverse needs of traders everywhere, whether you’re an experienced trader or just starting out.

    Standard Account

    Our MetaTrader 5 Standard account is suitable for both new and experienced traders.

    • Variable spreads
    • Market execution
    • No commission

    STP Account

    Our MetaTrader 5 STP account provides you with the tightest spreads and connects you directly to the market.

    • Variable spreads

    • No commission
