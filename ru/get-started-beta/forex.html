What is Forex trading

The foreign exchange market (Forex) is the world's most liquid and most traded market, where trades worth trillions completed each day.

Торговля на Forex подразумевает покупку одной валюты и одновременную продажу другой валюты. Поэтому данные валюты часто называют парами. Например, EUR/USD или GBP/USD.

Which currencies can I trade

Торговля на Forex предполагает покупку или продажу данных "валютных пар". Покупка пары EUR/USD означает, что Вы одновременно покупаете ЕВРО и продаёте ДОЛЛАР.

Currency pairs are categorised as follows:

  • Основные пары включают наиболее популярные мировые валютные пары
  • Вторичные пары – менее ликвидные валютные пары
  • Экзотические пары включают одну основную валюту (не являющуюся USD) против валюты развивающейся страны. Пример: GBP/HKD

The spread – why it matters

When you see currency pairs offered by a broker or trading service, there are usually two prices available: the ask price and the bid price. These are also known as the buy price and the sell price respectively.

The spread is the difference between the ask and bid price.

What time is the market open for me to trade

The Forex is an over-the-counter market where trading takes place between two parties, and not with a centralised exchange or marketplace.

Depending on your broker or trading platform, you can start trading from the time the Sydney market opens on Monday morning to the time the New York market closes on Friday evening – up to 24 hours a day, five days a week.

Клиенты Binary.com могут торговать на Forex с 21:00 ч. воскресенья по 21:00 ч. пятницы (по Гринвичу).

Как торговать на Forex

У трейдера Forex всегда есть основная задача: обменять одну валюту на другую с целью получения прибыли.

This is why we've come up with the following three-step tutorial to help you bridge that gap and make your first trade:

Step 1: Learn to read currency pairs

One of the first things most Forex traders learn is how to read a currency pair. There are two parts to a currency pair

EUR / USD

Основная валюта
Also known as transaction currency
Quote currency
Also known as counter currency

Tips

  • The base currency is always equal to one unit.
  • The bid price of the currency pair indicates how much of the quote currency is required to buy one unit of base currency. This is more commonly known as the exchange rate.

Например, если Вы видите, что цена бид пары EUR/USD составляет 1.05229, Вы продаёте USD 1.05229 (валюты котировки) на каждый EUR 1 (базовой валюты), которую Вы покупаете.

Если цена аск равна 1.05382, Вы купите 1.05382 USD за каждый проданный 1 EUR.

Step 2: Understand when to buy and when to sell

Think that a certain currency will go up or down? Learn when you should buy (or "go long") and when to sell (or "go short").

Traders choose to buy a certain currency pair if they think the value of the base currency will rise. The opposite is also true: they sell a certain currency pair if they think the value of the base currency will fall.

Let's compare the differences between buying and selling, using the EUR/USD as an example:

Покупка

  • You're buying the EUR and selling the USD
  • You expect the EUR to rise in value so you can sell it back for a profit
  • Buy = go long

Продажа

  • You're selling the EUR and buying the USD
  • You expect the EUR to fall in value so you can buy it back at a lower price (and make a profit)
  • Sell = go short

Step 3: How to purchase your first currency pair

Выбрав желаемый контракт, приобретите валютную пару на платформе MetaTrader 5.

Рассмотрим пример пары EUR/USD с ценой бид и ценой аск:

To go long, you'll want to click on 'Buy' to purchase EUR 1 for USD 1.17726

To go short, you'll click on 'Sell' to sell EUR 1 and receive USD 1.17725 in return.

Политика расчета маржи

Margin allows you to trade on leverage – meaning your existing capital can give you a much higher level of market exposure.

Например, если Вы хотите приобрести 100 единиц определённого актива, торгующегося у обычного брокера по цене USD 50 за единицу, данный контракт обойдётся Вам в USD 5 000.

Тем не менее, с кредитным рычагом вы сможете купить 100 единиц актива лишь за малую часть от их стоимости, а зависимости от размера кредитного плеча, предложенного брокером или торговой платформой.

What's a margin call and how is it applied

When your account equity is unable to cover the margin requirement, your account will be placed under margin call. This does not affect your ability to open new positions.

What's a stop out level and how is it applied

If your account is placed under margin call for an extended period of time, it will reach the stop out level where it is unable to sustain an open position. This will lead to your pending orders being cancelled and your open positions being forcibly closed (also known as "forced liquidation").

When your account hits the forced liquidation level, your orders and positions are forcibly closed in the following sequence:

  1. We analyse your pending orders
  2. We delete an order with the largest margin reserved
  3. If your margin level is still under the stop out level, your next order will be deleted. However, orders without margin requirements will not be deleted
  4. If your margin level is still under the stop out level, we will close an open position with the largest loss
  5. We will continue to close open positions until your margin level becomes higher than the stop out level

Как читать таблицу описания контракта

The Forex is typically traded in lots. One standard lot is equivalent to 100,000 units. Each time you open a position on a currency symbol, you can start with a minimum transaction of 0.01 lots.

Кредитный рычаг показывает, каким капиталом Вы можете управлять, в зависимости от размера Вашего первоначального взноса на счёт. Например, кредитный рычаг в размере 100:1 позволит Вам контролировать до USD 100 000, имея на счету всего USD 1 000.

Важные примечания к нашему курсу своп (перенос позиции на следующий день)

If you keep any positions open overnight, an interest adjustment will be made to your trading account as indication of the cost required to keep your position open.

This interest adjustment (or swap rate) is based on interbank lending rates, on top of a 2% fee.

The interest adjustment is calculated in 'points' – meaning we will convert the relevant interbank lending rates to 'points' in the base currency.

Please take note that our swap rate also depends on the time and days you hold your positions open:

  • Курс своп взимается, если Вы сохраняете позицию открытой после 23:59:59 GMT.
  • Positions that are still open on Wednesday at 23:59:59 GMT will be charged three times the swap rate to account for weekends – a standard practice for all Forex brokers.
  • Наш курс своп также может меняться с учётом праздников.
